National Prison Addiction Medicine Network

The National Prison Addiction Medicine Network brings together stakeholders across the sector to develop consensus guidelines for the management of addiction in custodial settings, and to promote evidence-based, best-practice healthcare for people in prison through a coordinated national approach.
